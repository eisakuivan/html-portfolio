The Best Movies of All Time For Real

Can't decide whether to go on a magical adventure or just chill with your friends?

Here are some ideas for your next movie night!

Spirited away (2001)

Hayao Miyazaki is a sorcerer. Watch this if you want to be whisked away to a fantasy world full of magic, masks, and baths!

Bridesmaids (2011)

Hilarious and heartwarming. Want to laugh and feel thankful for your friends at the same time? This movie is for you.

The Mummy (1999)

An action adventure with mummies and mythology? What else could you ask for?
